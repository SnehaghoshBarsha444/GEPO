Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEPO Pathway Program?

The GEPO Pathway Program is a brilliant opportunity for students at Universities to attain a master’s degree from a reputed international university. GEPO has partnered with top business schools across the US, UK, Germany, and other education hubs to offer comprehensive programs for brighter career prospects.

How are programs offered by GEPO different?

GEPO provides personalized counseling, international exposure, and placement support, making it unique among service providers.

What support services does GEPO provide?

GEPO offers career guidance, visa assistance, and ongoing academic support to ensure a seamless study abroad experience.
