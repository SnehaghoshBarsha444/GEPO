Frequently Asked Questions

Institutions can initiate partnerships by submitting a partnership inquiry form through our website. Our team will review the application and schedule a consultation to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

GEPO offers various programs including student exchange, faculty exchange, joint research initiatives, summer schools, and cultural exchange programs.

The partnership process typically takes 3-6 months, depending on the scope and complexity of the collaboration. This includes initial discussions, agreement drafting, and final approval from both institutions.
